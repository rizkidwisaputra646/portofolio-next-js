## Overview
This platform was designed to provide semantic search capabilities over millions of unstructured documents using Large Language Models (LLMs) and Vector Databases.

## The Problem
Keyword-based search was failing to provide relevant results for complex user queries. The system needed to understand intent and context.

## Implementation: RAG Architecture
We implemented a **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** pipeline:
1. **Embedding:** Documents are converted into vector embeddings using OpenAI's `text-embedding-3-small`.
2. **Storage:** Vectors are stored in Elasticsearch with k-NN search enabled.
3. **Retrieval:** User queries are embedded and compared against the document vectors.
4. **Generation:** The most relevant chunks are passed to GPT-4 to synthesize a natural language response.

## Technical Challenge: Latency
To keep response times under 500ms, we implemented a sophisticated caching layer and optimized the vector search indices.

## Results
- **85% improvement** in search relevance scores.
- Scaled to handle **10k queries/minute**.
- Integrated into 5 internal enterprise tools.

## Future Outlook
Moving towards agentic workflows where the search engine can autonomously perform multi-step research tasks.
